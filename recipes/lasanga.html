Ingredients List

  • Meat: 1 pound of ground meat(1/2 pound ground pork, 1/2 pound lean ground beef)
  • Onion: A diced onion is cooked until translucent with the ground meat
  • Fresh Herbs: Chop two tablespoons of parsley and crush one glove of garlic
  • Canned Tomatoes: 1 can of tamato sauce and 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • Spices and Seasonings: Dried basil, dried oregano, salt, sugar, black pepper
  • Noodles: Uncooked noodles
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese, parmesan, and shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs:Makes cheese layer extra cheesy!
